# MandalaPlan Installation Instructions

1. UNINSTALL the existing app from BOTH your Phone and Watch. This is crucial for the sync to work (Signature Mismatch Fix).
2. Install `MandalaPlan-Mobile.apk` on your Phone.
3. Install `MandalaPlan-Wear.apk` on your Watch.
4. Open the Phone app first, then the Watch app.
5. Watch app should show "Connected".
6. Record a timer session on Watch and Stop to sync.
